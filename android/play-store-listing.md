## Full description (max 4000 chars)

```
BuddyAlly is the local trust app for everything that happens between
people in your city — rides, events, favors, meet-ups, group plans, and
the everyday connections that make a place feel like home.

Why BuddyAlly?

• Real activities, not infinite scroll. See what's actually moving
  around you — pickup games, ride shares, study groups, neighborhood
  help — and join with one tap.

• People you can actually meet. Profiles show ratings, verifications,
  what they've hosted before, and trust signals from the Buddy Line —
  so you know who you're saying yes to.

• Share rides and split fuel. Going downtown, to the airport, to the
  next city? Post your route or find someone heading your way.

• Local help, lightning fast. Need a hand moving a couch, watching a
  pet, or grabbing groceries? Post once, neighbors respond.

• Group activities & events. Spin up a recurring run club, plan a
  weekend trip, or RSVP to what's already on. Built-in chat keeps
  everyone in sync.

• Safety first. Verified profiles, in-app safety protocols, easy
  reporting, and a community that's accountable. Your contact details
  stay yours.

• Free to use. No subscriptions, no ads in your face. BuddyAlly is
  free for everyday rides, help, and meet-ups.

How it works:
1. Sign up — pick your home area and what you're into.
2. Browse what's happening nearby in Explore.
3. Join an activity or post your own.
4. Connect, meet, and build your local circle.

BuddyAlly is currently active in Atlanta, Abuja, Lagos, and growing.
Invite your people and bring your city closer.

—

Questions? Suggestions? Email us at hello@buddyally.com or visit
buddyally.com to learn more.
```

## Tester access (optional but recommended)

Before going live to production, push a release to **Internal testing**
first. Add 3–10 trusted testers via email. They install via a Play Store
link and report bugs in TestFlight-style. No review delay for internal
testing — instant.

After 1–2 weeks of clean internal testing, promote that build to
**Production** with one click in Play Console.
